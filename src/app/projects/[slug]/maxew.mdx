maxew is a 2D platformer made over a 2-day game jam. The theme of the game jam
was "Reflect". We made a 2D platformer that was reflected across the middle of
the screen. Certain parts of the level can only be seen on the left or right
side.

- Made by: Me, Julia Ahopelto
- Made with: JavaScript, Phaser
