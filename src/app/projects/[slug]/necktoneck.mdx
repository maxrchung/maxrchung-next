Neck to Neck is a 2D platformer where you guide 2 heads to the top of the level. The heads are connected by a stretchable neck and constrained by its length. You can either play solo or set up a server to play over network. The game is loosely based on Japanese folklore.

- Made with: GameMaker
- Made by: Max Chung, Winston Liang, Derek Malonzo
